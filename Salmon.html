Salmon Recipe


 Ingredients Needed

  • Salmon
  • Herbes de Provence
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic butter

Instructions

  1. Dry. Use a paper towel to dry your salmon fillets. Double-check to make sure that there are no bones in the fish.
  2. Add the oil. Pour the oil on your fish, and rub it on both sides.
  3. Season. Mix the herbs and spices together and sprinkle them on both sides of the fish.
  4. Cook. Turn the air fryer on to 390F. Cook until the fish can easily be flaked with a fork. This should take 5-8 minutes.
  5. Melt the butter. While the fish is cooking, melt the butter in the microwave.
  6. Add the butter. When the salmon comes out of the air fryer, pour the melted butter over it, and serve immediately.
