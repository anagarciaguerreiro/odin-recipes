Whole Chicken Recipe


 Ingredients Needed

  • Chicken
  • Chicken stock
  • Garlic cloves
  • Seasonings
  • Lemon
  • Olive Oil Cooking Spray
  • Butter and/or oil

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken in your preferred way
  2. Mix the melted butter, fresh herbs, dried seasonings, and juice from half a lemon in a bowl. Using a brush, baste the outside of the chicken.
  3. Alternatively, you can add the seasoning directly to the chicken without using the butter.
  4. Pour the chicken stock directly into the Ninja Foodi bowl before transferring the seasoned whole chicken to the bowl.
  5. Place the pressure cooker lid on top and set the pressure cooker off using the cooking times listed below.
