Meatloaf Recipe


 Ingredients Needed

  • Ground beef
  • Bread crumbs
  • French onion soup mix
  • A bit of Ranch dressing mix
  • Dehydrated onions
  • Garlic powder
  • Egg
  • Barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. First you pressure cook your meatloaf so the meat gets done.
  2. Then you'll drain the water inside your pot, return the basket with your meatloaf inside and pour barbecue sauce on top.
  3. Cose your air fryer lid. That is the one that is attached.
  4. In 8-12 minutes the outside of your meatloaf will become wonderfully crusty like we like it here.
