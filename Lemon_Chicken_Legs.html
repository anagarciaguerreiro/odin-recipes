Lemon Chicken Legs Recipe


 Ingredients Needed

  • Chicken Legs
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh Lemon
  • Olive Oil Cooking Spray

Instructions

  1. Place the Chicken Legs in the Ninja Grill and lightly spray with the Olive Oil Cooking Spray
  2. With a sharp knife, cut a slice in the side of the Lemon, and squeeze the Lemon Juice directly onto the Chicken Legs.
  3. Season with salt, garlic an bleck pepper.
  4. Slice the Lemon, and lay the slices on top of the Chicken legs.
  5. Press the Grill button, and cook for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. When the Ninja Grill beeps, let the Chicken Legs sit for about 10 minutes, without opening the lid to the grill.
  7. After 10 minutes, open the lid to the Grill, and with tongs, place the Chicken Legs on a platter, and lay the Lemon slices around the platter.
